About me

Hello, I'm Harry. Welcome to my web page! I'm delighted to have caught your attention and that you've taken in interest in my coding skills. I am a self-taught web developer who is proficient in a wide array of web development platforms and languages. Take a look around and learn a little about what I can do for your project!

A little about me, I hail from the snowy lands of northern Michigan and currently reside in my hometown of Traverse City, right on the pinky of Michigan's lower peninsula. I got my bachelor's degree from Michigan State University in 2008, and in the years since I have worked as a press liaison on Capitol Hill and taught English in Seoul, South Korea.

I got into web development because I love technology and I believe in building great things that can improve people's lives and businesses. Just as much, I love the aspect of crafsmanship and skill building required to become truly proficient in coding languages.

Take a look at some of my work under the Portfolio section, and shoot me an email under Contact if you'd like to work together.
